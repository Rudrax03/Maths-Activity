An Activity on the Real-Life Application of Discrete Mathematics

Problem Statement

You are required to create a secure login page where the password must be alphanumeric and contain special characters. The Pigeonhole Principle is applied here to ensure uniqueness and security of user credentials.

Discrete Mathematics Principle Used

Pigeonhole Principle: If there are more items than containers, at least one container must hold more than one item. In terms of passwords, having a limited number of unique combinations with a large number of users means password collisions will occur without complexity.
